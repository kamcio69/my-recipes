Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

Poached eggs add so much texture and protein to meals. Don’t let them intimidate you—try it out! This dish is full of healthy fats and seasoned with a light sprinkle of basil.

Yield: 2 servings
Prep: 5 min
Cook: 5 min

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 4 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon basil
  • crushed red pepper flakes, to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a medium pot of water to a boil. Turn off the heat and crack eggs into 4 corners.
  2. Cover and poach 4–5 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  3. Toast bread. Spread avocado on toast.
  4. Top with eggs and seasonings.
