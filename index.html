Black Bean Breakfast Burritos

Warm and spicy breakfast burritos with black beans, eggs, sweet potatoes, avocado and spinach.

Yield: 6 servings
Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 oz) can low-sodium black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 10 large eggs
  • 6 whole wheat tortillas
  • 450 g sweet potatoes, cooked and mashed
  • 2 avocados, diced
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Season black beans with cumin, chili powder, and red pepper flakes.

  3. Warm tortillas and spread mashed sweet potatoes evenly.
  4. Add eggs, avocado, beans, and spinach.
  5. Season, fold, and roll into burritos. Serve immediately.
